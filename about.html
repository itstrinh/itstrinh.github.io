Overview

Hello! My name is Trinh Duong. I am currently a sophomore at the University of California, Berkeley majoring in Business Administration and working towards a Certificate in Entrepreneurship Technology. I will be graduating in the spring of 2018. Having grown up in a low-income community in the heart of Silicon Valley, I developed a strong interest in the crossroads of business, social impact, and technology.
